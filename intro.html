About

Project geodesy-domain-model is a key component of Geoscience Australia’s eGeodesy software infrastructure. It provides persistent domain entities and services modelling the core business data types and operations relevant (currently) to management of GNSS CORS site metadata.

The persistent domain entity model is based on GeodesyML.
